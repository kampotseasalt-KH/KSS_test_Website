Kampot fleur de sel blended with Label Rouge certified herbes de Provence from Aix-en-Provence. A meeting of two terroirs — the coastal salt pans of Cambodia and the sun-drenched hills of southern France. The herbs perfume without overpowering; the salt carries them with grace.

  • Label Rouge certified herbes de Provence — Aix-en-Provence, France
  • Hand-harvested fleur de sel from Kampot Province, Cambodia
  • Warm, fragrant, and beautifully balanced
  • No artificial additives or preservatives
